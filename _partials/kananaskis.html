Kananaskis Village

Getting There

Kananaskis Village is located approximately 90km west of the city of Calgary, about a 1-hour drive. This location is directly accessible by car. There is also a shuttle option from the Calgary airport.

We recommend carpooling with other guests. We've created this Facebook group to find others to carpool with. Please let us know there if you need or can offer somebody a ride.

When driving to or from the hotel, please be vigilant and watch for wildlife along Highway 40. This road is frequented by deer, bighorn sheep, and mountain goats.

Things to Do

There's no shortage of things to do in Kananaskis! Some ideas are listed below.

Take a Hike

Go hiking, literally go outside and walk around. Watch out for bears.

Waterpark! Waterpark!

As a guest at either the Kananaskis Lodge or Crosswaters Resort, you will have access to their waterpark and outdoor hot tub.

Yay Sports!

There are facilities to play tennis and basketball on location.

Kananaskis Nordic Spa

Take some time for some much needed rest and relaxation
